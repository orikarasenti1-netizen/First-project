Frequently asked questions

Is Folio really free?

Yes. Invoices are unlimited and free forever — no trial, no credit card, no signup. We make money when you upgrade to Pro for proposals and extra features.

Where does my data go?

Nowhere. All your invoices and proposals live in your browser's localStorage. We have no servers, no database, and no access to your data. It can't leak because we never see it.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, cancel any time from your Stripe billing portal. No questions asked. You keep Pro access until the end of your current billing period.

What's included in "no branding"?

Free plan PDFs include a small "Made with Folio" footer. Pro removes it entirely so your client only sees your name and work.

What happens to my data if I cancel Pro?

Your data stays in your browser. You'll drop back to the 3-proposal limit going forward, but nothing you've already created disappears.

Do you offer refunds?

If you're not happy within the first 14 days, email us and we'll refund you in full. After that, we handle refunds case-by-case.
